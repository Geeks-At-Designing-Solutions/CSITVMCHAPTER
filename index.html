SAC student council : Governance Model for the Chapter

As we have to build a strong student work force to trigger the CSI activities, applications are invited from creative and dynamic CSI student Execom members to be a part of the CSI Student Activities Committee, CSI Trivandrum Chapter. The student council is responsible to

  • nurture and retain student workforce in the frontline with professional skills
  • organize and promote National Tech Talk series
  • organize and promote State level student convention (SLC) /Regional level student convention (RLC) / National level conventions (NLC)
  • Outreach activities
  • Managing Web portal for reporting student activities
  • Assisting Trivandrum chapter in running certification programs.

In a way to channelling the student activities the following model was initiated by management committee for the smooth coordination with various student branches. It is not to be interpreted as over-riding the Bylaws of CSI.

SAC-Student Chair

The SAC is actively working on getting more students involved in the CSI Trivandrum Chapter. The Chair will be responsible for promoting SAC and its efforts towards various student branches. He/She will be formal point of student chapters. Encourage membership drive targeting different educational institution. Promote outreach activities among school/Hearing impaired students (NISH)/others.

SAC- Student Co-Chairs

Technical

This Co-chair is responsible for SAC Projects & Initiatives. The co-chair is also responsible for managing web portal/Web-based coding Competitions. He/She should assist Trivandrum chapter in running certified programs.

Innovation

This Co-chair is responsible for promoting collaboration with other societies by jointly organizing events (hackathon/online training for students.

Publicity

This Co-Chair is responsible for managing electronic communications as they pertain to SAC-organized activities. This will include maintaining an online presence as well as making announcements on social media platforms to promote the CSI and the activities of the SAC.

Design

This Co-Chair is responsible making /editing promo video /colourful flyers pertain to SAC-organized activities. Promote the SAC activities through Newsletters

Event

This Co-Chair is responsible for coordinating or organize events connecting different student branches. This co-chair will likely be expected to work closely with SAC for National FC Kohli Tech talk series /conference in organizing student-led sessions.

SAC -Student Vice Chairs

The student vice chairs shall assist the chair/Co-Chair and should be playing leadership role similar to co-chair. Vice chair will also be a formal point of contact of student chapters
